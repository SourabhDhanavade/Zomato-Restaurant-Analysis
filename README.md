<h2 align="center"> Zomato-Restaurant-Analysis </h2>
<strong>Project Title:</strong> Zomato Restaurant Analysis.<br> 

<strong>Description:</strong> Analyzed dataset covering 15 countries, 1825 cuisines, ratings, average costs, and city distribution. Collaborated in a 7-member team to perform data cleaning, exploration and visualization. Delivered insights on global food trends, pricing variations and customer preferences based on dashboard. 

<strong>Objective of the project:</strong><br>
⚫ Analyze dataset for zomato restaurants<br>
⚫ Transform restaurant data into actionable business insights using BI tools<br>
⚫ Develop interactive dashboards to support data-driven decision-making<br>

<strong>Findings:</strong>
<h3 align = "left">Map Visualization</h3>
<img align = "right" width="400" height="200" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/85df2e4d-18ca-4b65-a9d7-216c32c67285" />
This analysis explores Zomato’s presence across 15 countries and 141 cities by evaluating the distribution of 9,551 restaurants. Using map visualization in Excel, Power BI, and Tableau, we identified key regions with high and low restaurant density, revealing Zomato’s strong domestic presence and international reach. Zomato has a strong hold in Indian metros, especially New Delhi.Internationally, presence is sparse–indicating potential for targeted growth.<br><br><br>

<p align = "left"> <h3> Price Bucket</h3><p/>
<img align = "right" width="400" height="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/91aa6105-ec8b-4367-a5cd-e608f28ce283" />
<p align = "left">
Most restaurants (2700+) fall in the ₹201–₹400 price range.
₹0–₹200 and ₹401–₹600 also have over 2100 restaurants each.
After ₹600, the number of restaurants drops a lot.
People mostly prefer eating at budget-friendly places.
₹201–₹400 is the sweet spot – both affordable and popular.
Premium restaurants (above ₹800) are fewer – they serve a small group of high-paying customers.

Suggestions to improve sales:

Focus on ₹200–₹600 range: Give offers, combo deals, and use online delivery apps.
For ₹600+: Add value combos to attract more people.
For high-end restaurants: Highlight ambience, special dishes, and use social media influencers to attract rich customers.
</p><br>

<h3 align = "left" > Restaurant Ratings & Recommendations </h3>
<img align = "right" width="400" height="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c8630f0f-5911-4396-82ae-30e90c2a98ec" />
There is a high concentration of restaurants with poor ratings (1.0–1.5),suggesting quality or service issues.
Few restaurants achieve top-tier ratings, indicating room for improvement in customer satisfaction and experience.<br><br>
Recommendations for Zomato:<br>
1. Quality Audits<br>
2. Support Underperformers<br>
3. Boost Visibility for Top Performers<br>
4. Customer Feedback Loop <br>
5. Filter & Sorting Enhancement <br><br><br><br><br>

<strong>Insights:</strong><br>
🔹 Focus on improving ratings through service quality, hygiene, and customer feedback.<br>
🔹 Capitalize on popular cuisines and cost-effective segments for targeted campaigns.<br>
🔹 Utilize peak months like March, July, and September for new launches or promotions.<br>
🔹 Promote online delivery and table booking to boost reach and convenience.<br>
