<strong>Findings:</strong><br>
📍 This analysis explores Zomato’s presence across 15 countries and 141 cities by evaluating the distribution of 9,551 restaurants. Using map visualization in Excel, Power BI, and Tableau, we identified key regions with high and low restaurant density, revealing Zomato’s strong domestic presence and international reach. Zomato has a strong hold in Indian metros, especially New Delhi.Internationally, presence is sparse–indicating potential for targeted growth.<br>

<h3>Price Bucket</h3>

<div style="flex: 1;">

Most restaurants (2700+) fall in the **₹201–₹400** price range.

- ₹0–₹200 and ₹401–₹600 also have over **2100 restaurants** each.
- After **₹600**, the number of restaurants drops significantly.
- People mostly prefer eating at **budget-friendly** restaurants.
- **₹201–₹400** is the sweet spot—both affordable and popular.
- Premium restaurants (**above ₹800**) are fewer and cater to a smaller group of high-paying customers.

### Suggestions to Improve Sales

- **₹200–₹600:** Offer combo meals, discounts, and strengthen presence on food delivery platforms.
- **₹600+:** Introduce value-for-money combos to attract more customers.
- **Premium Restaurants:** Highlight ambience, signature dishes, and collaborate with social media influencers to reach premium customers.
